Welcome to Copper View Animal Hospital

Copper View Animal Hospital is proud to serve the Riverton UT area for everything pet related. Our veterinary clinic and animal hospital is run by Nathan Stefanoff, who is a licensed, experienced Riverton veterinarian.

Our team is committed to educating our clients in how to keep your pets healthy year round, with good nutrition and exercise. Copper View Animal Hospital stays on top of the latest advances in veterinarian technology and above all, remembers that all animals and pets need to be treated with loving care in every check-up, procedure, or surgery.
